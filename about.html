About TextUtil App

Welcome to TextUtil! This simple yet powerful web application helps you quickly manipulate and analyze your text. You can convert text to uppercase or lowercase, remove extra spaces, and copy your text effortlessly. Designed with simplicity and responsiveness in mind, TextUtil works perfectly on both desktop and mobile devices.

Whether you're a student, developer, or casual user, TextUtil makes text processing easy and efficient. Feel free to explore and use all the features!
